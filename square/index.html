Features

  • Each day's meal arrives in a sturdy square box which contains each course of the meal (main course, pudding, juice, snack) in a separate foil container. The top of the box lifts off to leave a ready-made tray for the food. This all-in-one square design means that diners don't have to keep getting out of their seat to fetch each course - it is all there ready.


  • The emphasis is on high-quality, well-sized portions of traditional 'square' meals (see the example menus below).


  • All ingredients are locally sourced and freshly made.


  • Each meal is high in nutrients, and the menu for each week is designed to provide diners with a good balanced overall diet.


  • Meals are tailored to each diner's requirements, for example allergies, likes and dislikes.
