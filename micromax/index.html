TNC study in India: Micromax

Micromax is a TNC (founded in India) which predominantly produces smartphones. Here is some information about them.

Positives of Micromax

feedback

Important Service

Micromax provides a highly important service of providing mobile phones for remote areas of India where landlines don't exist.

paid

Affordable Tech

Micromax provides affordable tech for the masses of ordinary consumers, offering many budget models for affordable prices. One of its latest models is one of India's cheapest 4G mobile phones.

design_services

Wide Range

They offer a wide range of products, providing a large choice for consumers to pick from.

electrical_services

Plugs Market Gaps

Micromax has been good at plugging gaps in the market and providing technology that is genuinely useful, like developing revolutionary phones with specially made long battery life to combat the problem of frequent power cuts in India.

perm_phone_msg

Call Features

They also developed another useful feature - the call recorder feature - after the co-founder, Rahul Sharma, met a painter who wanted his calls recording as sometimes clients would go back on their verbal agreements of price.

send

Good Target Audience

Micromax are good at targeting younger people with phone designs that appeal to them.

work

Provides Jobs

Products are made in India and provide Indian jobs.

emoji_events

Awards

Micromax has won many reputable awards: Emerging company of the year, and Transformational Business Leader of the year, 2017 (for Rahul Sharma)

sell

Russian Market

Micromax was the first mobile company to sell phones in Russia.

Negatives of Micromax

trending_down

Declining

Micromax is no longer the thriving company it was in the early 2010s when it was a major player in the field of mobile technology (second only to Samsung in India), but now suffers from competition, especially from China.

search_off

No Future

Its long-term prospects are suspected to be poor, considering the strength of larger Chinese companies who produce similar products for less money.

policy

Controversial

There have been questions over Micromax's business practices, and they have been successfully sued by Ericsson for infringement of their patents.

gpp_bad

Sneaky

In 2015, they were accused of stealthy installation of adware without permission which was later proven to be accurate.

attach_money

Bribery

In 2013, the co-founder of Micromax was send to prison after being caught paying bribes.
